Understanding Your SEO Score

Our SEO Checker, powered by Google's Lighthouse technology, provides a comprehensive audit of your website's technical health and user experience. A high score in these areas is crucial for ranking well in search results.

  • Performance: Measures how fast your page loads and becomes interactive. Core Web Vitals like LCP, CLS, and TBT are key factors here.
  • Accessibility: Checks if your website is usable by people with disabilities. This includes proper alt text for images, sufficient color contrast, and correct ARIA attributes.
  • Best Practices: Looks for modern web development standards that lead to a healthier and more secure site.
  • SEO: Audits for fundamental on-page SEO factors, such as having a `<title>` tag and a meta description, to ensure your site is understandable to search engine crawlers.
