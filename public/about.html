About FindAName.live

We believe a great idea deserves a great name. But we also know that the perfect domain can feel like a needle in a haystack. That's why we created FindAName.live.

Our Story

FindAName.live was born from a simple frustration shared by entrepreneurs, developers, and creators everywhere: the endless cycle of "type, search, taken." We spent hours, sometimes days, trying to find a domain that was short, memorable, relevant, and available. The creative spark for a new project would slowly dim with every "This domain is not available" message.

We knew there had to be a better way. With the power of modern AI, we saw an opportunity to transform the domain search from a frustrating chore into an inspiring creative process. We set out to build a tool that didn't just check for availability, but one that could act as a true creative partner, generating unique and brandable names you'd never have thought of on your own.

Our Mission: Empowering Your Next Big Idea

Our mission is to empower creators, entrepreneurs, and dreamers with the tools and knowledge to build a strong online brand, starting with the perfect name.

A domain is more than just a web address; it's the foundation of your brand's identity. That's why we don't stop at the name. We've built a comprehensive suite of free tools—from DNS and WHOIS lookups to an AI-powered Domain Value Calculator—to give you all the information you need to make smart decisions. Our blog provides expert insights into branding, SEO, and growing your online presence, ensuring you have the knowledge to turn your great name into a famous destination.

Our Philosophy

We believe that powerful tools should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their budget. That's why the core functionality of FindAName.live is, and always will be, 100% free.

We support our work through transparent affiliate partnerships with domain registrars and unobtrusive advertising. When you find a name you love and purchase it through our links, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This model allows us to keep the servers running and continue building new tools to help you succeed.
